New, simpler, beatiful and better mail list for the 21st century

Mail list for our century.

With gOrge we create a new, simpler and just better mail list system, it is now simpler to create a new discussion thread, add whover you like, share with everybody in the world your discussion; you won't need anymore to explain again and again to different person or team what decision are been made nor explain why, they can just read it by themself.

Beatiful

gOrge works with markdown, very simple and extremely powerful text format that let you espress any idea or format in manner of few character.

Simple

Very simple to use, you don't even need to sign up to start using the service, just leave your email, so the other participant will know who you are, and you can start your thread right now.

Better

gOrge is been designed to be simple to use, everybody can read the thread, and only the people who post need to be abilitate by the author of the thread.

Cheap

gOrge is free for everybody, also we provide a cheap plan so you can made your thread private and only the people authenticate can read.

Safe

We let our user to be verified, it is not necessary at all, but in this way participant at the thread will be sure about who you are.

Privacy

We don't read nor analyze in any way the threads, we don't sell commercial nor we care about who you are.
